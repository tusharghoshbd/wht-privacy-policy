# Privacy Policy

Work Hour Tracker ("we", "our", "app") respects your privacy.

## Information We Collect
- User account information (name, email)
- Work hour data (sign-in and sign-out times)
- Device information for app functionality

## How We Use Information
- To track work hours
- To generate reports
- To improve app performance

## Data Sharing
We do not sell or share personal data with third parties.

## Data Storage
Data is securely stored and accessible only to authorized users.

## User Rights
Users can request data deletion by contacting us.
